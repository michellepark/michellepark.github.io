What is pair programming?

Pair programming is where you program with another person, either remotely or face to face, working together to come up with solutions for a coding problem/challenge.

Compare navigator and driver

Navigator

The navigator guides the pair throught the steps necessary to successfully complete a challenge.

Driver

The driver is the person who is actually doing the typing.

Why is pair programming important to DBC?

Pair programming is important to DBC because it allows us to learn from each other, teaches us how to work well with someone else, and helps solidify our knowledge by teaching.
